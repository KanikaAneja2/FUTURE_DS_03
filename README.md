# Marketing Funnel & Conversion Performance Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes a bank marketing campaign to evaluate customer conversion performance across different stages of the marketing funnel. The analysis identifies where customers drop off, compares conversion rates across different customer segments, and provides actionable recommendations to improve campaign effectiveness.

---

## Objective

- Analyze customer conversion performance.
- Measure engagement, conversion, and drop-off rates.
- Identify high-performing customer segments.
- Compare conversion rates across job, education, contact type, month, and previous campaign outcome.
- Provide business recommendations for improving marketing campaigns.

---

## Dataset

- **Source:** UCI Machine Learning Repository – Bank Marketing Dataset
- **Records:** 41,188
- **Features:** 21
- **Target Variable:** `y` (Customer subscribed to term deposit: Yes/No)

## Key Insights

- Overall conversion rate is **11.27%**, indicating room for improvement.
- Engagement rate is nearly **100%**, showing that almost all customers were successfully contacted.
- Students and retired customers recorded the highest conversion rates.
- Cellular campaigns outperformed telephone campaigns.
- Customers with previous successful campaign outcomes were more likely to convert again.
- Conversion rates varied across education groups and campaign months.

---

## Recommendations

- Increase investment in cellular marketing campaigns.
- Prioritize high-converting customer segments.
- Re-target customers who responded positively in previous campaigns.
- Improve campaign messaging for lower-performing customer groups.
- Optimize campaigns during low-conversion months.
